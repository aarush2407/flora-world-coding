What are Strings?

In the previous level, we mastered numbers, but now we need to master human language. In Python, any sequence of characters inside quotes is called a String. One of the coolest things you can do with strings is called Concatenation. This is a fancy coding word for "gluing strings together." In Python, you can actually use the + sign to join two pieces of text into one. For example, "Hello" + " World" becomes "Hello World". You can also multiply strings!
