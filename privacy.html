Privacy Policy

At Flora World Coding, accessible from your GitHub URL, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Flora World Coding and how we use it.

Log Files

Flora World Coding follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. All hosting companies do this and a part of hosting services' analytics.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Flora World Coding uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.

Our Advertising Partners
